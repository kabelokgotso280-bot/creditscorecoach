How to Improve Your Credit Score

1. Pay All Your Accounts On Time

Your payment history makes up 35% of your credit score. Set reminders, debit orders, or early payments.

2. Keep Credit Usage Below 30%

If your credit limit is R10 000, try not to use more than R3 000. High usage lowers your score.

3. Avoid Too Many Credit Applications

Each application triggers a “hard inquiry” which drops your score. Only apply when necessary.

4. Keep Old Accounts Open

Older accounts increase your credit age, boosting your score over time.

5. Check Your Credit Report Regularly

Dispute errors, incorrect listings, or fraudulent accounts. Fixing them raises your score fast.

6. Reduce Your Debt Gradually

Pay down store accounts, personal loans, and credit cards. Even small payments help.
